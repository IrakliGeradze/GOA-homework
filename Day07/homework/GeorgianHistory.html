Vakhtang l Gorgasali

King Of Iberia

Vakhtang l, known as Vakhtang Gorgasali was born around 440 AD into the royal dynasty of the ancient Georgia Kingdom Of Iberia His Family ruled the influence of Byzantine Empire

The name "Gorgasali"means "Wolf Headed" ---- Gorgasali coming from Old Georgian words for wolf and head. According to legend he wore a helmet shaped like wolf's head, symbolizing courage and strenght.





Founder Of Tbilisi

Vakhtang founded Tbilisi, which later became capital of georgia

The story goes that while hunting near the Mtkvari River his falcon caught a pheasant, then both fell into a hot spring and died. Amazed by the warm water Vakhtang ordered a city to build here naming it Tbilisi from "Tbili" means warm in georgia




Vakhtang tried to balance realtions between Byzantines and Persians

At first he cooperated with the Sassanid Persians

Later, after tensions grew he allied with the Bynzantine Empire and rebelled against Persia to win



Death

Vakhtang died around 502 AD and he was burried in Mtskheta the ancient capital of Iberia in the Svetitskhoveli Cathedral.
